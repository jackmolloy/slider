How to Make Pour Over Coffee

by Jack Molloy

Step 1

Boil water in a gooseneck kettle

Step 2

Measure 4tbsp of whole coffee beans

Step 3

Grind beans to a fine, sand-like level

Step 4

Place filter and grinded beans into the V60

Step 5

Place V60 on scale and zero the scale

Step 6

Start timer and pour in a slow, clockwise pattern until reaching 48g. Let coffee bloom.

Step 7

Continue pouring in stages (70-100g at a time) until reaching 384g. This process should take about 3 minutes and 15 seconds.

Step 8

Remove filter, serve and enjoy!
